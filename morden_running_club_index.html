About the meetup

A simple local social run

This meetup is being organised to bring together local people who want company for regular exercise, without the pressure of joining a formal club.

It is intentionally informal and is not coaching, personal training, or medical advice. Participants should use their own judgement, choose a pace that suits them, and only join if the session feels appropriate for their own fitness and health.

Important notice: By attending, runners are joining a casual social meetup rather than a coached session or supervised fitness programme. Everyone takes part voluntarily and at their own discretion.
Good to know

FAQs

Do I need to be fit or experienced?
No. All abilities are welcome. This is intended to be friendly and low-pressure.
Is this coaching or a professional training group?
No. This is an informal social meetup, not coached training or a professional fitness service. The organiser does not hold running or fitness qualifications.
What if I am slow?
That is completely fine. Nobody is expected to race. The aim is to move, chat, and make it easy for people to join.
How will I find the group?
The organiser will be wearing a neon green T-shirt at the meetup point.
What should I bring?
Comfortable running shoes, weather-appropriate clothing, and water if you want it.

Safety and participation

Important before joining

Run at your own pace

This is a casual social meetup. There is no obligation to match anyone else’s speed or distance.

Use your own judgement

Only join if the session feels suitable for your current fitness, health, and confidence level.

Be sensible outdoors

Please obey crossings, traffic signals, and general road and pavement safety at all times.

Stop if needed

If you feel unwell, uncomfortable, or unable to continue, stop and look after yourself first.
