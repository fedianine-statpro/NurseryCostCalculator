        How to play

        Survive one calendar month. You and your rival each start with $1,000 on Day 1 and race to Day 31. Every turn moves you forward exactly one day — the choice is how you spend it.

        Each turn, pick one:

        • Work — a steady paycheck ($0–$250). Boring, reliable, safe.
        • Live it up — draw a life-event card. Could be a bonus check, a windfall inheritance, or a $750 natural disaster. Fortune favors the bold.

        Perks

        Twice per game you may draw a perk card. If you can afford it, buy it — they give one-time bonuses, passive income, or discounts on matching life events.

        Overdraft

        Balance below $0? You're forced to work, and the bank takes 10% of your debt each turn until you're back in the black.

        Win

        Be first to Day 31 with at least $1,000. If both of you qualify, the higher balance wins. Finish under $1,000 and you lose the month.
