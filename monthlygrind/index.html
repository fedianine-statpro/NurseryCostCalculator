        How to play

        Survive one calendar month. You and your rival each start with $1,000 on Day 1 and must reach Day 31.

        Each turn, pick one:

        • Work — draw a job and cash a paycheck ($0–$250).
        • Move — roll a die and advance. Landing on an event tile draws a life event (good or bad).

        Perks

        Twice per game you may draw a perk. If you can afford it, buy it. Perks give one-time bonuses, passive income, or discounts on matching life events.

        Overdraft

        Balance below $0? You must work, and the bank takes 10% of your debt as a fee each turn until you recover.

        Win

        Be first to Day 31 with at least $1,000. If both of you qualify, the higher balance wins. Finish under $1,000 and you lose the month.

        Hotkeys

        W work · M/Space move · P perk · Esc close
