TOP 10 RECOMMENDATIONS (Best overall for your trip)

1. Eweleaze Farm -- Dorset (Jurassic Coast)

  • Why: Private beach, campfires, sauna, cult favourite. Once-a-year opening makes it special.
  • Cost: ~GBP 144-200 | Drive: ~2.5h | Dates: Opens 20 Jul -- available 24-26 Jul or 31 Jul-2 Aug only
  • Book: eweleaze.co.uk

2. Wooda Farm Holiday Park -- Bude, Cornwall

  • Why: National Best Family Campsite Award. Hotel-standard facilities, Bude coast, great value.
  • Cost: ~GBP 100-130 | Drive: ~4.5h
  • Book: wooda.co.uk

3. Holden Farm Camping -- Hampshire (South Downs)

  • Why: Perfect 10/10 rating. Dark Sky Reserve for stargazing. Closest option to Morden at just 1.5h drive.
  • Cost: Contact directly | Drive: ~1.5h
  • Book: holdenfarm.co.uk

4. Westermill Farm -- Exmoor National Park

  • Why: Choose-your-own-spot by the River Exe on 500 acres. Individual firepits. Stunningly cheap.
  • Cost: ~GBP 60-80 | Drive: ~3.5h
  • Book: westermill.com

5. Quarry Park Camping -- Pembrokeshire

  • Why: Near-perfect 4.99/5 rating. Every pitch has its own firepit & BBQ grill. Award winner.
  • Cost: ~GBP 120+ | Drive: ~4.5h
  • Book: quarryparkcamping.co.uk

6. Draughton Heights -- Yorkshire Dales

  • Why: Highest-rated campsite on Pitchup (10/10, 251 reviews). Immaculate facilities.
  • Cost: ~GBP 120-160 | Drive: ~4.5h
  • Book: Via pitchup.com

7. Cummins Farm -- Lyme Regis, Dorset

  • Why: Working sheep farm, campfires, fossil hunting at Charmouth/Lyme Regis. Brilliant value.
  • Cost: ~GBP 56-112 | Drive: ~3h
  • Book: lymeregiscampsite.co.uk

8. Woodlands Grove -- Dartmouth, Devon

  • Why: FREE entry to Woodlands Family Theme Park with 10 play zones and zoo farm. Unbeatable for kids.
  • Cost: ~GBP 100-180 | Drive: ~3.5h
  • Book: woodlandsgrove.co.uk

9. Ladram Bay Holiday Park -- East Devon (Jurassic Coast)

  • Why: Private beach, indoor pool, giant pirate ship playground, soft play, entertainment. All-in-one.
  • Cost: ~GBP 90-180 | Drive: ~3h
  • Book: ladrambay.co.uk

10. Notgrove Holidays -- Cotswolds (Glamping)

  • Why: Best glamping value. Ensuite pods with kitchen on a 1,500-acre estate. Flexible arrival days.
  • Cost: ~GBP 220-260 | Drive: ~2h
  • Book: notgroveholidays.com

IMPORTANT BOOKING NOTES

  1. Book immediately. July is absolute peak season. Small sites (Ty Parke: 12 pitches, Quarry Park: 17 pitches, Little Haven: 15 pitches) will sell out fast.

  2. 17-19 July is likely the best weekend -- just before school holidays begin (~21 July), so slightly lower prices and better availability.

  3. Minimum stay rules: Some sites enforce 3-night minimums from late July:

    • Trewan Hall: 3-night min from 20 July
    • Pencarnan Farm: 3-night min in peak
    • Pentewan Sands: week-long bookings for premium pitches in peak

  4. Eweleaze Farm opens 20 July, so only available for the later two weekends.

  5. Isle of Wight sites require budgeting GBP 100-180 extra for car + passengers return ferry.

  6. All prices are estimates based on published 2024-2026 rates. July peak pricing may be higher. Always confirm directly before booking.

  7. Electric hook-up typically adds GBP 5-12/night. Extra adults, children, and dogs add GBP 2-15/night each.
