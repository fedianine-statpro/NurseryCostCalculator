Government Childcare Funding: Eligibility & Limitations

The government provides funding for 15 to 30 hours of childcare per week for eligible children. Key points to consider:

  • Eligibility: Available for children aged 3 to 4 years.
  • Duration: 38 weeks per year, aligning with the term time.
  • Additional Costs: Funding covers only the educational service. Costs like meals, nappies, and trips may be extra.
  • Provider Policies: Each childcare provider may apply the funding differently based on their policies.

For full details and criteria, please consult the official government childcare funding guidelines.
